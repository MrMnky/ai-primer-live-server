How It Works
Foundation models are trained on enormous datasets ‚Äî sometimes trillions of words ‚Äî to build a general understanding of language, logic, and the world. Think of it like giving someone a university education in everything simultaneously. The foundation model itself is never used directly by end users. Instead, it's fine-tuned, prompted, or augmented to create the products you actually interact with (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

Why This Matters

Understanding the ecosystem helps you make better decisions: which model for which task, when to use off-the-shelf vs fine-tuned, and why "AI" isn't one thing ‚Äî it's a stack of capabilities you can mix and match.
